about

METFIELD INVENTIONS LLP is a trading and service company that deals with aluminium and lead alloys all over the world. We have the special Know-How from the raw material to the end product. Many yeras of expirence on the metal market with a great customer and supplier base were perfect preconditions to start our business in the year 2010.

We also work for the biggest trading companies as a agent in Europe, middle East and Asian countries. We constantly improve ourselve and try to find the best and shortest way to communicate with our suppliers and customers. Because only with the right reaction and close customer care it´s possible to satisfy our customers. These preconditions are the best one for a long-term and successful cooperation between our customers and suppliers.

Today we bring all necessary services to the end user such as

- Just in Time logistic

- Exchange and currency hedging

- Warehousing

- Customer solution
